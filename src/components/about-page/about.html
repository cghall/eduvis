About

Eduvis presents education data in a visually compelling way. It enables users to assess how individual schools are performing on a range of metrics and to observe broader trends within the education system.

It is designed to be completely customisable, giving users full control over the data and how it is presented. The Share functionality makes it easy to share insights with others.

The secondary school data view uses data from the Department for Education and Ofsted, all of which is publicly available.

If you have suggestions for how Eduvis can be improved please get in touch with us at info@eduvis.co.uk.
